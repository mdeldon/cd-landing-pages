# AdQuadrant Bid Recommendations - Citizens Disability

**Date:** March 15, 2026  
**Prepared by:** Morgan (Media Buyer)  
**Campaign Type:** Google Search - SSDI Lead Generation

---

## Executive Summary

These recommendations cover two distinct lanes targeting adults 50+:
- **Lane 1:** Reconsideration (denied at initial level)
- **Lane 2:** Hearing scheduled

Revenue potential differs significantly between lanes, warranting separate strategies.

---

## Lane 1: Reconsideration (Denied at Initial)

### Revenue Context
- **Avg revenue per signup:** ~$1,839
- **Max CPL (break-even):** ~$900
- **Target CPL (profitable):** $600-$750

### Recommended Bids

| Metric | Recommendation |
|--------|----------------|
| **Max CPC** | $8.00 - $12.00 |
| **Target CPA** | $700 |
| **Daily Budget** | $150 - $250/day |

### Bid Strategy
- **Primary:** Manual CPC with smart bidding assist (Target CPA $700)
- **Secondary:** Maximize Conversions (if volume needed)
- **Start conservative** - these leads convert at lower rates than hearing lane

### Rationale
Reconsideration cases are harder to win (lower conversion rate). Aggressive bids here eat margin. Start at $8-10, optimize to $12 only if CPL is sustainable.

---

## Lane 2: Hearing Scheduled

### Revenue Context
- **Avg revenue per signup:** ~$2,400 - $5,700
- **Max CPL (break-even):** ~$1,200 - $2,400
- **Target CPL (profitable):** $900 - $1,400

### Recommended Bids

| Metric | Recommendation |
|--------|----------------|
| **Max CPC** | $15.00 - $25.00 |
| **Target CPA** | $1,200 |
| **Daily Budget** | $300 - $500/day |

### Bid Strategy
- **Primary:** Target CPA ($1,200) with max CPC cap at $25
- **Secondary:** Maximize Conversion Value (if CPA bidding underperforms)
- **Can be more aggressive** - higher revenue per signup = more bid room

### Rationale
Hearing-stage leads convert at higher rates and pay out more. Worth paying up to get these.

## A/B Test Ideas

### 1. Match Type Split
- **Test:** Exact vs Phrase vs Broad (modified) for each lane
- **Hypothesis:** Exact may have higher CPL but better quality; Broad captures volume
- **Split:** 50/50 traffic initially

### 2. Ad Copy Angle Test
- **Test A:** Emphasize "Already Denied? We Can Help" (recon lane)
- **Test B:** Emphasize "Hearing Scheduled? Win Your Case" (hearing lane)
- **Hypothesis:** Stage-specific messaging should improve CTR and conversion

### 3. Landing Page Test
- **Test:** Single-form vs Multi-step qualification
- **Hypothesis:** Multi-step may filter better but reduce volume

### 4. Bid Strategy Test
- **Test:** Manual CPC vs Target CPA vs Maximize Conversions
- **Run for 2 weeks minimum** before declaring winner

## Optimization Checklist

### Week 1-2: Learning Phase
- [ ] Let campaigns learn (don't over-optimize)
- [ ] Monitor search terms closely - add negatives
- [ ] Review CPCs vs impression share

### Week 3+: Optimization Triggers

| Metric | Action |
|--------|--------|
| CPL > target by 20% | Reduce max CPC 15%, tighten keywords |
| CTR < 1% | Test new ad copy, review keyword intent |
| Conversion rate < 3% | Check landing page, add qualification questions |
| Budget hitting cap | Increase budget 20% if CPL is acceptable |
| Quality Score < 5 | Improve ad relevance, landing page experience |

### Key KPIs to Track
- **CPL** (primary)
- **Conversion Rate** (from click to lead)
- **Cost per Click** (watch for overbidding)
- **Impression Share** (are we missing volume?)
