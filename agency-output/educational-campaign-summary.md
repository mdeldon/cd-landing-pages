## Campaign Strategy

### Pre-Denial Funnel (Lane 0)
Target: People who haven't been denied yet
- Educational content builds trust
- Capture email early
- When denied → retarget to Lane 1

### Pre-Hearing Funnel (Lane 2)
Target: People who got hearing date
- Hearing prep content
- When hearing scheduled → retarget to Lane 2
